Stonewoods, Resort & Spa

Laced with all the basic facilities and amenities that make life easy, this holiday retreat proves to be one of the best resort to visit. In addition, Spa and ayurvedic massage centre, family cottages and other delightful offerings including Mandrem Food court makes it a wonderful place to relax and revel in the magical environment of Goan style.

Our well organized 32 cottages are fully prepared to welcome tasteful travelers and provide the well deserved stay with modern amenities food from every continent and take on a journey with us; towards total relaxation and rejuvenation while indulging in the therapeutic yet luxurious SPA treatments.

Dine

The famous Mandrem Food Court is a part of the hotel where we serve global cuisine ranging from Russian, Arabic, Chinese, Mexican, Continental and Indian cuisine at one place. The food can be enjoyed with live entertainment like live band, belly dance, magic show etc. on different days of the week.
